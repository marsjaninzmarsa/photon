Message bar

Message bars are used to communicate information to the user. The message bars show at the top of the page or section they apply to.

Usage

Message bars contain a concise and scannable text directly related to the action performed or to be performed. They may contain contain a one or more actions, neither of which may be Dismiss or Cancel. They always contain a icon before the message.

Message bars can be used to display different kinds of information such as success messages, updates, warnings and problems.

Anatomy

Icons

Icons may provide additional clarity. Icons should be placed to the left of a title or message. These glyphs (16x16) can be found in the icons library.

Title

In case the message you're trying to comunicate it's really complex, you can use a title. When possible, communicate the main message using just the title.

Message

The body of the message should be contained within two lines. Be descriptive and include any troubleshooting actions.

Actions

Add one or two buttons in the Messabe bar when you want users to take an action after reading the message.

Dismissable

Unless they contain critical information, message bars are dismissible by clicking on the ghost button, placed always on the right corner.

Sizes

Dimensions

Message bars have a fixed min-height and width may vary depending on the content. It cannot grow more than its parent container.

Typography

Message bars use Body-10 for the message. Title-10 for the Title.

Icons

Icons always have a 4px padding all around.
