Emphasis

The type scale enables you to set the hierarchy of your content. If two texts feature the same scale use a bolder font weight 500 to emphises the most important information.

Color

Choose text color based on purpose. Primary for titles, buttons and most important bodies of text. Secondary for accompaingn text that gives further information, details or features to the primary content such as caption texts.
